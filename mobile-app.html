We have enabled the scheme to use your smart mobile to scan QR codes. You can download and try our Eco Rewards APP by Transmach for iPhone and Android. Existing members should activate the APP with their registered ID or smart card number. New members can enter their e+ card number or select “none” if they have not been given a smart tag or ID. Contact us if you have a questions about the scheme.

You can see locations of our QR signs and readers on our locations page.

Please go to App Store or Google Play, search for Eco Rewards, download, install and open the APP. When registering select Bracknell Forest resident trial or your community as appropriate.

Google https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.ecoreward
Apple (IOS) https://apps.apple.com/in/app/eco-rewards/id1634208068

Install on Google Play Install on App Store

Enter your usual return distance in miles for work, study or leisure.

You can then report green journeys by scanning our QR codes on signs and at blue readers. Each time you scan a QR code, we use your entered default journey details to calculate your green miles and carbon saving. You can report additional journeys with the self-reporting form.

Please let us know any problems and if it is working OK. If you cannot install or run the APP, contact us.
