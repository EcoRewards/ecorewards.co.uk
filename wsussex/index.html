How does it work?

Participants in the scheme register with our Mobile APP or online. Green journey are recorded by scanning the QR signs or tapping a smart tags, rail smartcard or existing Bracknell e+ cards on the Eco Rewards blue readers. Follow this link for the Mobile APP and to register. Smart Tags are issued by schools and other participating organisations.

Read about our Mobile APP or register on the Active page.

Or

Participants can also report their green journeys via the Aoo or an online form. We will calculate your CO2 savings and green miles. Over time, participants will see their carbon savings grow, both as an individual and as a member of the community.

You will be entered into prize draws and are eligible to access retailer discounts and cashback. You can see your points, savings and access to benefits by completing logging on to your account.

Discover our local offers and access discounts & cash-back www.ecorewards.org.uk.
