How does it work?

Once you have an activated smart tag or card, tap on a Eco Rewards reader as you travel to and from work, school or college. We will calculate and total your points, CO2 savings and green miles.

You can see your points, savings and access to benefits by completing your registration and logging on to your account. If you are joining through a school, community or business we will be running prize draws and calculating you group savings.

Get started

For Eco Rewards through Bracknell Forest school, contact your co-ordinator or email us

For Eco Rewards trial in Bracknell town, you can use you e+. Just tell us your normal travel mode and distance.

For commute trial via Bracknell station, you can use you rail smartcard. Just tell us your normal travel mode and distance.
