Eco Rewards for Businesses, their Staff and Community

Eco Rewards offers incentives and benefits for employers, employees and your community:

EcoWalk We can set up special walks, step challenges and competitions for businesses. Use our App to record walk to work and for leisure. Get fit and active with Eco Rewards.

EcoCycle - Cycle-to-work salary purchase scheme, cycle maintenance and discounts through our local bike partners. We can also encourage use of local Cycle Routes.

EcoCar We can offer a salary sacrifice lease scheme for Electric Vehicles via a reputable and established business partner Octopus EV, and we can also introduce employers to EV charger discounts on installation and service contracts. Follow link to find out more

EcoShare Report car-sharing journeys using our mobile App.

EcoBus Report bus journeys using our mobile App.

EcoRail Report rail journeys with GWR and SWR by scanning the QR signs at the stations and using our mobile App.

EcoChallenge We offer a series of motivational walking and cycling challenges with cash prizes to get businesses and employees engaged

Eco Rewards All active participants can see their own carbon savings, green miles and achieve virtual certificates in our App and benefit from regular cash prize draws and competitions as well as discounts and cash-back from over 2000 online retailers. Prizes will be linked to goals, level of participation and improvements.

Prizes will be linked to goals and improvements and awarded for participation, effort and winning challenges. We motivate members to realise their goals and award special virtual certificates on our App for outstanding achievements.
