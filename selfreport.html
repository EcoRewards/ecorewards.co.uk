If you have an Eco Rewards ID, smart tag or registered smartcard, you can use this form to report individual green journeys in the past 7 days and up to 3 different modes a day.

If you make more than one journey on the same mode, just report the total distance for that day.

To see your carbon savings and access benefits, visit https://www.ecorewards.org.uk and logon to your account. If you have any questions or feedback, please contact us.

Any duplicate or excess reports will not be added to your account.

Optional - please provide evidence such as picture of ticket or screenshot from walking app. Any images submitted must not contain personal information, illegal content or inappropriate content*. To establish and demonstrate confidence in self-reporting, we would love to see more detail about your journey and any walking-cycling apps you are using. Images will be held anonymously and archived or deleted after 30 days.
